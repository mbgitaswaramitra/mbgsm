About Us

The Gita Swara Mitra Marching Band is an extracurricular activity at SMK Mitra Industri MM2100, which is focused on students who have an interest in the world of music, particularly in Marching Band. This extracurricular Marching Band was established in 2011 when SMK Mitra Industri MM2100 had its first batch of students, and it remains active to this day.

One Band, One Sound, One Soul is our slogan at the Gita Swara Mitra Marching Band, which means Satu Band, Satu Suara, Satu Jiwa. Interested in becoming a member of MBGSM?

Extracurricular

Mengembangkan bakat dan minat siswa dalam bidang musik, khususnya marching band. Meningkatkan disiplin dan kerja sama tim antar anggota. Menumbuhkan rasa percaya diri dan kepemimpinan siswa. Mengharumkan nama sekolah melalui berbagai macam perlombaan dan pertunjukan.


Kegiatan selama melaksanakan ekstrakurikuler marching band :

  • Latihan rutin: Melatih teknik bermain instrumen, formasi marching, dan gerakan koreografi.
  • Pertunjukan: Melakukan pertunjukan dalam berbagai acara, seperti upacara bendera, festival seni, dan acara sekolah lainnya.
